Our Team

Research Group Members

Dr. Jiseon Yang

Position: Principal Investigator

Research Focus: Microbiology in Space, Bacterial Pathogenesis

Dr. Yang leads the research group, focusing on understanding microbial interactions in space environments and their implications for human health during long-term space missions.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Position: Postdoctoral Researcher

Research Focus: Microbial Genomics, Bioinformatics

Dr. Johnson specializes in analyzing genomic data from space-exposed microorganisms to identify genetic adaptations to microgravity.

Michael Chen

Position: Ph.D. Student

Research Focus: Biofilm Formation in Microgravity

Michael's research investigates how microgravity affects biofilm formation and antibiotic resistance in pathogenic bacteria.

Emily Rodriguez

Position: Lab Manager

Expertise: Molecular Biology Techniques, Lab Operations

Emily ensures the smooth operation of our lab, manages resources, and assists in various research projects.
